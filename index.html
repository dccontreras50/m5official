About

M5 Alien is an award-winning artist, a founder of the VR Conceptual Art and Tears Crystal Lattice Painting. Her visual practice explores the complex relationship between art & human beings unconscious.

Awards

Cannes Lions 2009-10, Film Lions, finalist (x2)
The Webby Awards 2014, honoree
London International Awards 2013, finalist
Golden Drum Festival Portoroz 2007-12, Gold, Silver, short list (x3)
Busan Int Advertising Festival 2009-12, Silver, Bronze, short list (x9)

M5 Alien’s works have been profiled by multiple publications, including Сreative Review and Lürzer’s Archive Magazine; broadcasted on TV, including CNN and CBS.
